About Us

Sel Florist's journey started towards the end of 1990 with what still remains its flagship flower shop. In the early years, Sel Florist was a little florist yet there was a great potential. Directors had a vision and decided to to persist and invest every resource into their plan for the business. This dedication to customer service and passion for the business has led Sel Florist from being just your local flower shop, to a recognised London brand.
